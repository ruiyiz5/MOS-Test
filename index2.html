In this project, we use Text-to-Speech models to synthesize Chinese speech. For each sentence, we first present the original human generated speech. Then, we present the speech audio synthesized by 5 different models. Please select one of the following scores for each audio according to its quality:

  • 2 (Poor) Annoying distortion, but not objectionable.
  • 3 (Fair) Perceptible distortion, slightly annoying.
  • 4 (Good) Slight perceptible level of distortion, but not annoying.
  • 5 (Excellent) Imperceptible level of distortion.
If you do not want to evaluate some sentences, please just skip them and the default value indicates you did not answer that question.
